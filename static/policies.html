📜 Government Schemes & Yojnas for Farmers

PM-Kisan Samman Nidhi

Direct income support of ₹6000 annually to farmers in three installments.

Eligibility: Small and marginal farmers.

Official Website

Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)

Crop insurance scheme to protect farmers against losses due to natural calamities.

Eligibility: All farmers growing notified crops.

Official Website

Soil Health Card Scheme

Provides soil nutrient status and recommendations to improve productivity.

Eligibility: All farmers.

Official Website

Kisan Credit Card (KCC)

Offers farmers timely credit for cultivation and other needs at low interest rates.

Eligibility: Farmers engaged in agriculture and allied activities.

Official Website

National Agriculture Market (e-NAM)

Online trading platform for agricultural commodities to ensure fair prices.

Eligibility: Farmers, traders, and buyers registered on e-NAM.

Official Website

Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchai Yojana (PMKSY)

Focuses on irrigation efficiency and water conservation for better crop yield.

Eligibility: Farmers across India.

Official Website

Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana (RKVY)

Provides financial support to states for agricultural development projects.

Eligibility: Farmers through state government programs.

Official Website

Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana (PKVY)

Promotes organic farming practices and certification support.

Eligibility: Farmers adopting organic farming methods.

Official Website

Gramin Bhandaran Yojana

Supports construction of rural godowns for safe storage of produce.

Eligibility: Farmers, cooperatives, and NGOs.

Official Website

National Mission on Sustainable Agriculture (NMSA)

Promotes climate-resilient farming practices and resource conservation.

Eligibility: Farmers adopting sustainable practices.

Official Website
